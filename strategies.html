Teleoperated

Our robot is equipped with the capability to collect notes from both the ground and the source. Utilizing a swerve drive and a custom-built neural network, it can precisely aim and shoot while in motion by continuously calculating the optimal arm angle and flywheel speed.

Endgame

Our endgame strategy is to be able to climb onto the stage with our polycarb climbers. We utilize our arm system to pull the robot up onto the chains.
